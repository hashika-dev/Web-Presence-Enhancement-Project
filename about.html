About M4ng4

M4ng4 was founded in 2025 with the aim of bringing the best of anime and manga culture to the Filipino community and beyond.

Our History

Starting as a small, fan-driven community, M4ng4 began as a place for Filipino anime enthusiasts to gather and share their favorite titles.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer an engaging, user-friendly experience for anime and manga fans worldwide, with a special emphasis on Filipino viewers.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: We believe anime is for everyone.
  • Community: We value the relationships built among our users.
  • Quality: We are dedicated to offering the best viewing experience.
  • Passion: M4ng4 was founded by fans, for fans.
  • Cultural Appreciation: We aim to introduce global audiences to the beauty of Japanese culture through anime and manga.
